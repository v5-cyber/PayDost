🔒 Security & Data Privacy

End-to-End Protection: PayDost uses state-of-the-art security to keep your business data safe.

Authentication: All sessions are secured with highly encrypted JSON Web Tokens (JWT). Your passwords are hashed and salted using bcrypt, meaning even we cannot read them.

Data Storage: Your data is stored locally in an encrypted SQLite database securely on your local server. It is NOT shared with third parties. Your financial information, client details, and project records are entirely private to you.
